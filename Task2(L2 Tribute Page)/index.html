Mr. Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi is an Indian politician and the current PRIME MINISTER OF INDIA, serving in this position since May 2014. Born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat.


"Achhe Din Aane Wale Hain" (Good days are ahead)

"Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat" (One India, Great India)

Personal Information

Narendra Modi was born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, a small town in the Mehsana district of Gujarat, India. He was the third of six children born to Damodardas Mulchand Modi and Hiraben Modi. His father was a tea-seller and his mother was a homemaker. Modi attended primary school in Vadnagar and completed his secondary education in nearby Mehsana. He then moved to Ahmedabad to pursue his higher education, where he obtained a Bachelor's degree in Political Science from the University of Gujarat. Modi is known to be a fitness enthusiast and practices yoga daily. He also enjoys reading and has written several books. In his free time, he likes to spend time in nature and has a passion for photography. Modi is known for his disciplined and hardworking nature. He is a good orator and has the ability to connect with people from different backgrounds. He is also known to be a perfectionist and pays attention to even the smallest details.

Modi as a Politician

In his early years, Modi worked as a volunteer for the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), a Hindu nationalist organization. He later became a full-time worker and rose through the ranks of the organization, serving in various positions. In 1987, Modi joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and was appointed as the General Secretary of the party's Gujarat unit. He was instrumental in organizing the party's campaign for the state assembly elections in 1990, which resulted in a victory for the BJP. In 2001, Modi was appointed as the Chief Minister of Gujarat, a position he held for four consecutive terms until 2014. As Chief Minister, Modi implemented several development projects in Gujarat and transformed the state into a business-friendly destination. In 2014, Modi led the BJP to a resounding victory in the general elections and was sworn in as the Prime Minister of India. During his tenure, he has focused on implementing various economic and social reforms, including the Make in India initiative, the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, and the Digital India campaign, among others. Modi's leadership style has been both praised and criticized. He is known for his strong communication skills and his ability to connect with the masses, but he has also been accused of being authoritarian and divisive. Despite this, Modi remains a popular leader in India, with a strong base of support among the country's Hindu nationalist and conservative communities.

Achievements

As the Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi has achieved several notable accomplishments. Some of his key achievements include:-

  • Economic Reforms:

    Modi has implemented several economic reforms aimed at boosting growth and creating jobs. These include the Goods and Services Tax (GST), the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, the Atmanirbhar Bharat initiative, and the Make in India campaign.


  • Digital India:

    Modi has launched the Digital India campaign, which aims to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. The campaign includes initiatives such as the BharatNet project, which aims to provide high-speed broadband connectivity to rural areas, and the DigiLocker initiative, which provides a secure and paperless way for citizens to store and share their documents.


  • Swachh Bharat Abhiyan:

    Modi launched the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission) in 2014, which aims to make India a clean and hygienic country. The campaign includes initiatives such as the construction of toilets in rural areas, the installation of public waste bins, and the promotion of cleanliness and hygiene.


  • Foreign Policy:

    Modi has strengthened India's foreign relations and increased its global standing. He has made several high-profile visits to other countries, including the United States,China, and Japan, and has initiated programs such as the International Yoga Day and the International Solar Alliance.


  • Jan Dhan Yojana: Modi launched the Jan Dhan Yojana in 2014, which aims to provide financial services to the unbanked population in India. The program has resulted in the opening of millions of new bank accounts and the distribution of debit cards to millions of citizens.

Initiavtives by Modi

Narendra Modi is known for his powerful speeches and inspiring quotes. Below are some of his famous initiatives:-

  • Make in India

    This is a campaign aimed at promoting manufacturing in India and attracting foreign investment in the country.


  • Digital India

    This is a program aimed at transforming India into a digitally empowered society and promoting digital literacy and e-governance.


  • Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

    This is a nationwide cleanliness drive launched in 2014 aimed at improving sanitation and cleanliness in the country.


  • Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

    This is a financial inclusion program launched in 2014 aimed at providing banking services to the unbanked population in the country.


  • Ayushman Bharat

    This is a healthcare program launched in 2018 aimed at providing health insurance to the poor and vulnerable sections of the society.


  • Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao

    This is a campaign aimed at promoting the education and empowerment of the girl child and improving the sex ratio in the country.


  • Skill India

    This is a program aimed at developing the skills of the Indian workforce and providing them with better employment opportunities.


  • Startup India

    This is a program launched in 2016 aimed at promoting entrepreneurship and innovation in the country.


  • Ujjwala Yojana

    This is a scheme launched in 2016 aimed at providing free LPG connections to households below the poverty line.
